# memogen Brand Presentation

Your interactive brand showcase presentation is ready!

## Setup Instructions

### 1. Save Your Logo Images

Save your logo files to the `images/` folder with these names:

```
images/
├── logo-white.png         # White logo for dark backgrounds
├── logo-navy.png          # Navy logo for light backgrounds
├── signage-mockup.jpg     # Outdoor signage photo
├── commission-mockup.jpg  # "Protect Your Commission" mockup
├── defaults-mockup.jpg    # "Reduce Defaults" mockup
├── elephant-icon.png      # Just the elephant icon
└── wordmark.png           # Just the "memogen" text
```

### 2. Image Requirements

From your logo concepts, you'll need:

- **logo-white.png**: Export your white version of the full logo (elephant + text)
- **logo-navy.png**: Export your navy version of the full logo
- **signage-mockup.jpg**: Save your outdoor signage photo
- **commission-mockup.jpg**: Save the window/poster mockup with "Protect Your Commission"
- **defaults-mockup.jpg**: Save the billboard mockup with "Reduce Defaults"
- **elephant-icon.png**: Export just the elephant icon by itself
- **wordmark.png**: Export just the "memogen Business Continuity Intelligence" text

### 3. Open the Presentation

Once images are in place, open `index.html` in your browser.

## Navigation

- **Click** anywhere (right side) to advance
- **Click** left 20% of screen to go back
- **Keyboard**: Arrow keys, Space, Enter, Backspace
- **Progress bar** at top shows your position
- **Slide counter** at bottom right
